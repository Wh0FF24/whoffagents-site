# Wingman Launch Plan - BYU Campus

## Product Overview
**The Wingman** - AI-powered dating assistant that analyzes screenshots of conversations and suggests response options tailored to different dating styles (Confident, Playful, Thoughtful).

**Target Market:** BYU students (dating-focused culture, tech-savvy)
**Pricing:** $5 per analysis
**USP:** Real-time conversation help when you're stuck, preserves your authentic voice

## Messaging Strategy

**Primary Hook:** "Never overthink a text again"

**Pain Points to Address:**
- Fear of saying the wrong thing
- Ghosting after bad response
- Overthinking every word
- Losing interest due to slow replies

**Benefit Positioning:**
- Confidence boost
- Authentic voice preservation
- Time-saving (instant suggestions)
- Improved dating success

**Tone:** Casual, relatable, slightly humorous (not sleazy)

## Backup Plans

**If flyers don't work:**
- Pivot to pure digital (Reddit + TikTok)
- Run Instagram influencer campaign (micro-influencers with 1k-5k followers)

**If pricing resistance:**
- Offer free trial (1 free analysis, then $5)
- Bundle pricing (3 analyses for $12)

**If privacy concerns arise:**
- Add "We don't store screenshots" badge
- Create privacy policy page
- Show data deletion after analysis
